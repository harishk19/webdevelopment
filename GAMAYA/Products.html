GAMAYAS customer relationship management (CRM) solution helps you find new customers, win their business, and keep them happy by organising customer and prospect information in a way that helps you build stronger relationships with them and grow your business faster. CRM systems start by collecting a customer's website, email, telephone, social media data, and more, across multiple sources and channels. Customer feedback is the information, insights, issues, and input shared by your community about their experiences with your company, product, or services.
